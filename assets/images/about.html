About

Learn more about the person behind ITSMBrew

Hi, I'm Elad. Welcome to my about me section!

Professionally, I bring nearly 20 years of experience in IT Service Management (ITSM) with expertise across multiple ITIL versions and a genuine passion for the field. What I find most fascinating about this discipline is that while ITIL provides excellent frameworks and best practices, there's never just one "right" answer.

Every organization has its own unique DNA—different resources, needs, capabilities, and timelines. Throughout my career, I've used my ITIL training as a valuable template to address organizational challenges, but the real magic happens between the lines: collaboratively working through problems and implementing solutions.

I've learned that perfection rarely happens on the first attempt—sometimes it's never fully achieved—but the value comes from iteration, adaptation, and continuous improvement. This approach has allowed me to deliver effective ITSM solutions tailored to each organization's specific context.

I hope you find value in the articles across this site. If you'd like to connect, feel free to reach out via email.
